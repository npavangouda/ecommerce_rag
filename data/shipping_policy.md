# Shipping and Delivery Policy

## Delivery Time

-   Standard delivery: **3--7 business days**
-   Express delivery: **1--2 business days** (available in select
    cities)

## Shipping Charges

-   Free shipping on orders above ₹500
-   ₹50 charge for orders below ₹500

## Order Processing Time

-   Orders are processed within **24 hours**
-   Orders placed on weekends are processed on next working day

## Delivery Delays

Delivery may be delayed due to: - Weather conditions - Public holidays -
High order volume

## Order Tracking

Customers can track orders using: - Tracking link sent via email/SMS -
Order history in account dashboard

## Failed Delivery

If delivery fails: - 2 re-attempts will be made - After that, order will
be returned to warehouse

## International Shipping

-   Currently not supported
