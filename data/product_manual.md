# Smart Device User Manual

## Getting Started

### Device Setup

1.  Press and hold the power button for 3 seconds
2.  Follow on-screen instructions
3.  Connect to Wi-Fi network

### Charging Instructions

-   Use only the provided charger
-   Full charge takes approximately 2 hours

## Safety Instructions

-   Do not expose device to water
-   Avoid extreme temperatures
-   Do not use while charging if device overheats

## Basic Operations

### Restarting the Device

-   Press and hold power button
-   Select "Restart" from menu

### Resetting the Device

-   Go to Settings \> System \> Reset
-   Confirm reset action

## Troubleshooting

### Device Not Turning On

-   Ensure battery is charged
-   Try holding power button for 10 seconds

### Slow Performance

-   Close unused apps
-   Restart the device

### Connectivity Issues

-   Restart Wi-Fi router
-   Reconnect network in settings
