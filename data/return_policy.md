# Return and Refund Policy

## Return Eligibility

Customers can return products within **30 days** of delivery.

To be eligible for a return: - The product must be unused and in
original condition. - The product must be in original packaging. - A
valid proof of purchase is required.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned: - Perishable goods - Personal
care items (e.g., earbuds, grooming tools) - Digital products and
software - Items marked as "Final Sale"

## Return Process

1.  Initiate return from your account dashboard.
2.  Select reason for return.
3.  Schedule pickup or choose self-ship option.

## Refund Policy

-   Refunds are processed within **5--7 business days** after
    inspection.
-   Refund will be issued to original payment method.

## Damaged or Defective Products

If a product is damaged or defective: - Report within **48 hours of
delivery** - Replacement or full refund will be provided

## Exchange Policy

-   Exchanges are only available for size or variant changes
-   Exchanges depend on product availability
